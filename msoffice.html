MS Office Course

Course Overview

The MS Office course is designed to provide students with practical knowledge of Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. It helps learners become proficient in office productivity tools widely used in education, business, and professional environments.

Duration

2 Months

Key Subjects

  • MS Word – Document creation and formatting
  • MS Excel – Data analysis, formulas, charts
  • MS PowerPoint – Presentation design and delivery
  • Office Productivity Tools – Shortcuts and efficiency tips

Eligibility

Open to all students with basic computer knowledge.

Career Opportunities

After completing this course, students can work as office assistants, data entry operators, administrative staff, or use their skills for freelancing and personal productivity.

Benefits of MS Office Course

  • Improves efficiency in office tasks
  • Boosts employability in administrative and clerical jobs
  • Helps in freelancing (data entry, report writing, presentations)
  • Enhances academic performance with professional reports and presentations
  • Provides lifelong skills useful in any career
