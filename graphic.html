Graphic Design Course

Course Overview

The Graphic Design course is designed to teach students how to use modern design tools and software to create professional logos, posters, and digital artwork. It focuses on creativity, visual communication, and practical skills for the design industry.

Duration

6 Months

Key Subjects

  • Adobe Photoshop – Image editing and manipulation
  • Adobe Illustrator – Vector graphics and logo design
  • CorelDRAW – Creative design projects
  • Poster, Banner, and Social Media Design

Eligibility

Open to all students with basic computer knowledge and interest in creativity.

Career Opportunities

After completing this course, students can work as graphic designers, freelancers, social media content creators, or pursue careers in advertising and digital marketing.

Benefits of Graphic Design Course

  • Develops creativity and artistic skills
  • Opens freelancing opportunities in design platforms
  • Helps in creating professional logos and branding
  • Boosts employability in advertising and media industries
  • Provides skills for social media content creation
