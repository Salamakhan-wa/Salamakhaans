Diploma in Information Technology (DIT)

Course Overview

The Diploma in Information Technology (DIT) is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer systems, programming, networking, and database management. This course equips learners with practical skills to pursue careers in IT support, software development, and system administration.

Duration

1 Year (2 Semesters)

Key Subjects

  • Computer Fundamentals
  • Programming Basics (C/C++)
  • Database Management Systems
  • Networking Essentials
  • Web Development (HTML, CSS)
  • MS Office Tools

Eligibility

Matriculation or equivalent qualification.

Career Opportunities

After completing DIT, students can work as IT technicians, junior programmers, database assistants, or continue higher studies in Computer Science.

Benefits of DIT

  • Hands-on training with real-world projects
  • Strong foundation for higher studies in IT
  • Improves problem-solving and analytical skills
  • Opens doors to freelancing and remote work opportunities
  • Boosts confidence in using modern technology
  • Enhances employability in both local and international markets
