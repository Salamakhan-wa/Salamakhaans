Certificate in Information Technology (CIT)

Course Overview

The Certificate in Information Technology (CIT) is designed for beginners who want to learn the fundamentals of computers and office applications. It provides essential skills required for everyday use of technology in education, business, and personal life.

Duration

6 Months

Key Subjects

  • Computer Basics & Operating Systems
  • MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint
  • Internet & Email Usage
  • Basic Troubleshooting

Eligibility

Open to all students with basic literacy skills.

Career Opportunities

After completing CIT, students can work as office assistants, data entry operators, or use their skills for freelancing and personal productivity.

Benefits of CIT

  • Perfect for beginners with no IT background
  • Improves office productivity and efficiency
  • Helps in freelancing (data entry, typing jobs)
  • Boosts confidence in using computers and the internet
  • Foundation for advanced IT courses like DIT
